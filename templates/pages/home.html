Our Menu View Specials

For over 14 years, Tasty Table has been the hot spot for all things food.

When we first opened as a catering company we saw the potential to please businesses along the Main Line. From corporate events to fundraising functions, we provided hospitality and a variety of hand made dishes to satisfy all. With the current environment, we decided to adapt to your needs. We are proudly now, Tasty Table Market. With no line and no hassle, come in to find hand picked speciality food items and vegetables along with prepared foods and sandwiches made by our incomparable chef team.

With Love,
Owner George McLoughlin & the Tasty Table team.
